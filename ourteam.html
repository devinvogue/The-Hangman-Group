BOARD OF DIRECTORS

EXECUTIVE / FOUNDER


PAUL ATTA OPPONG Paul holds a HND in Entrepreneurship & Finance from Kumasi Technical University and a Bsc in Accounting from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology and Certificate in Banking from SIMA Ghana. Over the years, Paul has been involved with numerous projects involving youths, including the work of St. Peter’s Cathedral Basilica Youth Council. He is the face of the Finapple, on the road meeting and visiting with every teen and young adult in places throughout Kumasi. He is responsible for understanding the needs of the finapple, gathering critical research and data to allow us to create the right programs that will drive growth and success for the group. Paul also handles growth strategies and identifying the right opportunities for continued growth. Paul has a long history of helping youth within the Kumasi metropolis. Paul is currently a Relationship Manager at PAK Financial Services and CEO of OPA Printing & Imaging Solutions
